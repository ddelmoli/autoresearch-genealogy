# Review Card: DNA Chromosome Analysis

Prompt: [12 DNA Chromosome Analysis](../prompts/12-dna-chromosome-analysis.md)

## Good Output

- DNA evidence is labeled as a lead unless documentary records support it.
- Small segments and ethnicity percentages are treated conservatively.
- Segment notes preserve uncertainty and possible alternative branches.
- Living match privacy is protected.
- The output proposes documentary follow-up questions.

## Red Flags

- A small segment is assigned to a specific ancestor without corroboration.
- Ethnicity percentages are mapped directly to one ancestor.
- Living match names are exposed in public text.
- The AI treats provider ethnicity labels as fixed historical categories.
- Phasing, segment size, and match quality are ignored.

## Verify Manually

- Check segment size, match reliability, and whether the data is phased.
- Compare the lead against documented family lines.
- Look for records that could support or disprove the relationship.
- Use [Verify An AI Finding](../checklists/verify-an-ai-finding.md).

## Reject The Result When

- The claim rests on ethnicity alone.
- Multiple branches could explain the same segment.
- A living match's private details would need to be exposed.
