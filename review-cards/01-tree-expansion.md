# Review Card: Tree Expansion

Prompt: [01 Tree Expansion](../prompts/01-tree-expansion.md)

## Good Output

- New people are added only with source notes.
- Living or possibly living people are skipped.
- Each added relationship has date, place, and family-context support.
- Failed searches are logged in `Research_Log.md`.
- Speculative leads stay marked as `speculative`.

## Red Flags

- A new ancestor comes from one user-contributed tree.
- Same-name matches are treated as proof.
- The output adds people without explaining the source.
- Living people are searched or expanded.
- The tree grows quickly but source quality does not improve.

## Verify Manually

- Open each source yourself.
- Confirm the person fits the known date and place.
- Check spouse, children, parents, witnesses, occupation, or religion when available.
- Use [Before You Add An Ancestor](../checklists/before-you-add-an-ancestor.md).

## Reject The Result When

- The source is missing or does not support the claim.
- The only support is an unsourced online tree.
- Another same-name person is plausible.
- The relationship is inferred from surname alone.
