# Review Card: Unresolved Persons

Prompt: [06 Unresolved Persons](../prompts/06-unresolved-persons.md)

## Good Output

- Each unidentified person is tied to the document where they appear.
- Candidate identities include evidence for and against.
- Witnesses, neighbors, informants, and sponsors are not automatically made relatives.
- Unknown people remain unresolved when evidence is insufficient.
- Follow-up searches are specific.

## Red Flags

- The AI turns every witness into a family member.
- Same-name candidates are accepted without date or place support.
- A nickname is treated as a legal identity without confirmation.
- Ambiguous people are merged into known ancestors.

## Verify Manually

- Re-read the source document.
- Check whether the person appears in census, church, probate, or land records near the family.
- Look for repeated witnesses across multiple records.
- Use [Verify An AI Finding](../checklists/verify-an-ai-finding.md).

## Reject The Result When

- The proposed identity depends on name alone.
- The person would be too young, too old, or in the wrong location.
- The source does not state the relationship.
