# Review Card: Local History Extraction

Prompt: [09 Local History Extraction](../prompts/09-bygdebok-extraction.md)

## Good Output

- Extracted claims include page, section, or image references.
- Original spellings and local place names are preserved.
- The output separates translated meaning from original text.
- Relationships from local histories are treated as compiled evidence.
- Conflicts with primary records are flagged.

## Red Flags

- The AI modernizes names without keeping originals.
- Page references are missing.
- A local history claim overrides a vital or church record without explanation.
- Farm names, patronymics, or house names are flattened into modern surnames.

## Verify Manually

- Open the page or image yourself.
- Confirm the transcription or translation.
- Compare extracted family groups against primary records.
- Use [Verify An AI Finding](../checklists/verify-an-ai-finding.md).

## Reject The Result When

- The page reference cannot be found.
- The text names a different family group.
- The extraction hides conflicting dates or relationships.
